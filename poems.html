Your eyes speak

We once dreamed of being two stars in our own vastness. Now I'm trapped in a desolate line of life. Day and night feel so strange without you. The innocence and the madness of the love we shared still sleep inside my eyes. You were my only desire; you were that place where peace met my soul. I loved you endlessly, but you will never understand. My impatient, anxious heart only wants to see you, and you don't realize how deeply in love I was. Sometimes tears break open in my eyes, and desperation gets stuck in my throat. You turned your back on someone who already carried too many wounds. I guess now I'll just be a broken, wandering star, traveling through the infinite spaces where we once lived.

A shadow trying to bloom

On my lips lives the kiss that never came to be, and in my voice a prayer for your return. I saw you sitting in my dreams, you looked like an angel— I was hallucinating, my eyes refused to part from you. My hope burns, and my heart is trapped inside the smoke. I am a forgotten shadow— where do I find fertile ground to be born again? Perhaps this is a punishment? How do I explain that your love is a flower trying to bloom in my barren fate? My heart has broken before, but without you my life lies down in a river of tears— and I don’t know if I can cross it.

Return color to my shadowed world

Don’t you see it, my love? You pushed my destiny into the void. My melancholy waits inside the box of our memories. Come now, silence this sadness— come now and wrap me in your skin. What can I tell my heart, when your absence soaks it in fear? Bring the colors back to my shadowed world. Come here— you are my only desire.

Broken under your absence

Love, why did you surrender me to loneliness? You’re not here, and my mind feels lost. Let me cry for you, let me miss you— what else can I do? All I gained were false promises, words already dead. All this anger, all this rage now camouflaged in tears… and all the love that vanished— you held the reins, my life. That illusion of kissing you beneath the veil and eternal fire— all of it forgotten. You take the best of me and leave me defeated. The memory of your gentle smile, my beloved, waits for me every night. You were only mine, I was only yours— and everything shattered. Distance will make you forget me, but it will wound me every single day.

You were mine until you weren´t

My love, you are no longer mine. What was my mistake? Neither my affection nor my time were ever enough. Where did our precious moments go? Pieces of my heart are scattered across my room. My love— my soul searches only for your name. Whisper to me again that you are mine. My skin refuses to wander into another’s warmth. Make me yours again. Do you not remember the madness that fell upon us? Do you never think of the way love used to hold us? Your love felt so entirely mine, and my love was so completely yours.

The storm you left at my door

Were you ever truly mine? My love, look closely— you left my soul in ruins. You forgot to take with you the moments you once gave me. Take everything that ever bound us together. I will gather, in pain, the pieces of our unfinished story. Perhaps you’ll never understand the greatness with which my eyes once looked at you. My love, you will never know the wounds I carried in my loneliness. Would you even have the strength to face that agony? If only your skin were my skin— if only you would stop and listen to me, just for a moment, my love— perhaps then you would understand the storm you left at my door.

What your silence reveals

Angel, how can you say so much with your silence? Did your heart hide itself behind a curtain of apathy? Or were you always this way? I’m left confused— your lies made me wait for you. So many promises, so many dreams… was it all truly a lie? Now it’s getting late, and waiting has become a poison. How do I hold on to reality? Perhaps time will place my words inside your eyes— perhaps my silence will make the truth speak. You can hide, but you will feel my absence. Somehow, somewhere, you will see my name— because my heart always feared your leaving.

We once melt for each other

Do you remember when we melted into each other? When our damp eyes could understand everything without a single word? What happened to your heart, that it no longer answers the way it used to? Does the echo of my love no longer resonate within your space? My whole world used to disappear into the pleasure of your voice… What happened, my love? Why does everything feel so numb now? My ears keep rewinding your laughter— I’m trapped day and night in your memory, my life. Is this the emptiness that grief leaves behind? Darling, look through your notes— you’ll find our story there again
