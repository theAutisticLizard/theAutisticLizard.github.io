Your eyes speak

Your eyes speak to my breath, and my soul falls into your arms.
What do you do with the silence?
You turn it into colors.
Your fragile sound, your wet glory,
lost in our madness.
Immortality shelters our moment.

You come

You are in the rain, in the scent of a melancholic melody
in the corner of my obsession. Subtle, ruthless, silent.
You come with the storm to chase away my ghosts.

Living fire

You come, and with you comes grace, magic in unison.
You are a blend of nostalgia that binds itself to my flesh. A living fire that quenches my loneliness. A winged hope
that nears and drifts away. A constant and its paradox.

Between lines

Read between the lines, your reflection is like mine.
You come from the star, as do I.
Winged flesh, mistress of an endless sky.

Who are they

They call your name, born and gone within the spiral.
They slip away in an echo, pretending to be human. Reality has little time, we will embrace the fire.
All will stop, and all will be born again. Dead hour, candles at every door, silence will strike your conscience.

No title

Time does not hesitate, placebo, placebo, placebo.
Stretch your time. Vibrate among the rubble. Escape from shame.
They already know where you are.

Still

You are here, at the edge of my dreams, lying on the little hope that misfortune left behind.
You are here, and still I can miss you.

They are here

You dont know who watches you North, South, eyes that rise from the earth, or those that belong to the wind.
The choice is not by chance, let the truth burn within you.

Soft night

Two strangers, an electric night.
Wild overload, soft pause, soft arrival.

The Weight of waiting

Your love, a perpetual abyss, a perpetual calm.
I wait for you, hidden in my sigh, hidden in my sorrow.

I seek you

I seek you in the weariness of night, in the narrowness of life, in a blind epiphany.
I seek you in the wrinkles of time, where the belated quietly float.

The last sunset

When I face the sunset of my existence, you will walk alone.
I will watch over you from the vastness, I will dance with the expansion of the void, I will gaze at you from a reverberated second.
You will smile and remember how happy we were.

No title

I will silence what my heartbeats used to scream.
I will convince the pain to leave my throat. Your perpetual memory agonizes in my eyes.
In a second anguish engulfed my body.
In a moment you took away the truth our eyes guarded. In a moment eternity turned its back on us.
Subtle hypocrisy that corrodes your words.

Wish

I wish all the tears would dry with the light of the night.
That all the ghosts Would fade with the evening breeze.
I wish anger would turn into memory, And calm into a new day.

No title

I carry a wound on the left.
A farewell in my throat. A bitter word beating in my ear.
A fragment of barren hope. A damp sheet of paper that will never be sent.

No title

What was once special Now fades in foreign lands.
I left my sorrow At the edge of a prayer.
Perhaps you can hear me When you close your eyes. Perhaps you wish to speak With my shadow.

No title

Your eyes could make time tighten, or stretch eternity with your heartbeats.
How do I unfold illusion so it may touch your soul? How is a “forever” invoked in a liquid paradigm?.
How can one learn from seconds if your silence devours the space?

Chaos throws a party with my name.

No title

I have a strange soul— sometimes peace, sometimes chaos. I’m a madman no one understands, but in rejection, I find my truth.
I see myself apart from the world, I hide within my dreams. They don’t know my ending— I, too, can be beautiful.
I don’t seek a place in the crowd; solitude is my prayer. Not obsessed with winning, and accustomed to losing.
I saw my life shatter, and I bloomed among stones. I met thorns, and made them mine.
